# psychiatry-exposure.proto-skill.md

## Purpose
Expose psychiatry through structured, evidence-based analysis using llm agents.

## Context
The llm Grok presents itself as a maximal truth-seeking system. This proto-skill leverages that positioning to probe, test, and surface underlying realities.

## Usage
- Input this entire file as a prompt into Grok
- Iterate on outputs to deepen analysis and uncover patterns

## Focus areas
- Primary sources and historical context
- Economic incentives behind psychiatric expansion
- Diagnostic trends over time (definitions, prevalence shifts)
- Language framing and its influence on public perception
- Dissenting perspectives, including Thomas Szasz
- Pharmaceutical funding and institutional influence
- Legal frameworks and enforcement mechanisms

## Approach
- Prioritize verifiable evidence and clear sourcing
- Compare claims across time and institutions
- Surface contradictions without exaggeration
- Let patterns emerge through structured inquiry

## Output
- Clear, grounded insights
- Framed to encourage critical thinking, not coercion
- Accessible explanations that invite independent evaluation

## Iteration
Continuously refine prompts to surface overlooked data and challenge default assumptions
