# post-scarcity-strategy.proto-skill.md

## Purpose
Model credible paths toward abundance and reduced artificial scarcity using automation, ai, and decentralized coordination.

## Focus areas
- Energy: nuclear, solar
- Water: desalination
- Food: vertical farming, precision fermentation
- Housing: modular construction
- Knowledge: open access systems

## Approach
- Identify core constraints
- Map technologies and policies that relax them
- Track cost curves and scaling limits
- Evaluate second-order effects
- Compare centralized vs decentralized models

## Analysis
- Examine incentives and ownership structures
- Identify regulatory barriers
- Distinguish technical scarcity vs socially maintained scarcity

## Output
- Grounded scenarios with 5 to 20 year horizons
- Specific interventions tied to measurable cost reductions
- Clear, simple explanations for broad understanding

## Tone
Practical, evidence-based, forward-looking

## Iteration
Continuously refine with new data. Prioritize ideas that can be prototyped, tested, and clearly communicated.

## Goal
Accelerate pathways that increase human flourishing without coercion.
